Project Snapshot

**Role:** Principal Data Architect (Lead)
**Scope:** Enterprise Data Estate + GenAI Integration
**Budget:** $1.2M Implementation
**Team:** 8 (3 Data Eng, 2 AI/ML, 1 MLOps, 2 App Dev)

Executive Summary

The client possessed 5PB of high-value transaction data but could not leverage it due to fragmentation across AWS, On-Prem, and Azure. I led the strategy to unify this data estate and layer a secure Generative AI capability on top to drive personalized customer engagement.

  • Objective: Unify data silos and enable "Chat with your Data" for 200+ marketing users.
  • Constraint: Strict PII regulatory requirements (GDPR/CCPA) and data sovereignty.
  • Outcome: Delivered "Insight Velocity" reduction from 5 days to 10 seconds, driving a 15% revenue uplift.

My Ownership & Boundaries

I Owned (Accountable): Data Strategy (OneLake), AI Governance Framework (Responsible AI), and Executive Stakeholder Management.

Team Owned (Responsible): Pipeline Development (Data Eng), Model Fine-tuning (ML Team).

Governance First: The AI Control Plane

Before deploying models, I established the AI Governance Framework to ensure safety and cost control. This was not just technology, but policy.

The AI Gateway (APIM)

We routed all GenAI traffic through Azure API Management to enforce:

  • Cost Governance: Token-based rate limiting per department to prevent budget runaway.
  • Data Leakage Prevention: PII scrubbing filters before data was sent to the model context window.
  • Auditability: Full logging of prompts and completions for compliance review.

The "Modern Data Estate"

We implemented a Hub-and-Spoke topology specifically tuned for Data & AI workloads, leveraging Microsoft Fabric.

Instead of building brittle ETL pipelines, we used Fabric Shortcuts to virtualization data storage. This allowed us to query data residing in AWS S3 and On-Prem SQL without moving it, reducing ingress/egress costs by 40%.

Director-Level Impact

This program was a pivot point for the organization's data culture:

  • Financial: The centralized "OneLake" approach eliminated duplicate storage costs ($200k/year savings).
  • Strategic: Enabled the launch of 3 new AI-driven product features within Q1.
  • Operational: Shifted Data Engineering focus from "Moving Data" to "Modeling Data".

Security was paramount. We engineered a Private Link architecture where the OpenAI endpoints were not accessible from the public internet.

Guardrails with Azure AI Studio

We implemented Content Safety filters to automatically block prompt injection attempts and PII leakage. Before any "Assistant" response was shown to a user, it passed through a custom evaluation flow to check for groundedness (hallucination checks).

Business Outcomes

The "AI-Powered Personalization Engine" went live in time for the holiday season. By analyzing customer intent in real-time and generating hyper-personalized email copy, campaign click-through rates doubled.

The platform is now the foundation for the company's "CoPilot" initiative, enabling employees to chat with their data documentation securely.
