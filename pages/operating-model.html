Delivery Excellence

How I Run Delivery

Architecture without governance is just a suggestion. I implement rigorous operating models that bridge the gap between technical strategy and business value.

Architecture Governance

Establishing Architecture Review Boards (ARB) and Technical Review Boards (TRB) to ensure ensuring every design decision aligns with enterprise standards (CAF) and security baselines before a single line of code is written.

Migration Wave Planning

Structuring complex migrations into "waves" based on application affinity and business criticality. Moving from "Pilot" (Low Risk) to "Production" (Mission Critical) to minimize blast radius.

Risk Management (RAID)

Proactive management of Risks, Assumptions, Issues, and Dependencies. I maintain a live RAID log that is reviewed weekly with stakeholders to unblock delivery teams.

The Delivery Lifecycle

1. Discovery & Assessment

Using strict criteria to assess workload readiness. Deliverables: Assessment Report, TCO Analysis.

2. Foundations (Landing Zone)

Deploying the "Paved Road". Identity, Network, and Security baselines via IaC (Terraform/Bicep). Deliverables: Hub-Spoke Topology, Policy Set.

3. Migration & Modernization

Executing the waves. Rehosting (Lift & Shift) or Refactoring (PaaS). Deliverables: Cutover Plan, Test Reports.

4. Handover & Operations

Ensuring Day-2 readiness. Knowledge transfer to Ops teams. Deliverables: Runbooks, Monitoring Dashboards.
