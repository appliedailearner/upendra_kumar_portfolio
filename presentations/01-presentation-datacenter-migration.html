Strategic Data Center Migration

Modernizing Infrastructure for Agility & Cost Efficiency

Architecture Deck

Executive Summary: The Challenge

01

Context: The organization faced a critical hardware refresh cycle with costs projected to jump 40% YoY. Legacy on-prem infrastructure was stifling innovation.

  • Risk: End-of-Life (EOL) hardware and Unknown Dependencies causing outage fears.
  • Financial: High CapEx requirements for new data center lease.
  • Operational: Manual provisioning taking weeks, not minutes.

Urgency

Data Center Lease Expiry:
8 Months

Target Architecture: Enterprise-Scale

02
  • Identity: Extended AD to Azure via Domain Controllers in Identity Subscription.
  • Connectivity: Hub-and-Spoke topology ensuring traffic segregation via Azure Firewall.
  • Management: Standardized Policy assignment (Tagging, RDP Deny) at Management Group scope.
  • Landing Zone: Deployed via Terraform (ALZ-Bicep) for repeatable subscription vending.

Migration Process Deep Dive

03

1. Decide & Plan

  • Business Case: Calculated TCO (CapEx vs OpEx) to justify move.
  • Discovery: Deployed Azure Migrate Appliance for agentless inventory.

2. Assess

  • Dependency Mapping: Visualized multi-tier app connections to avoid breaking changes.
  • Right-Sizing: Optimized VM SKUs based on actual performance history.

3. Migrate & Manage

  • Test Failover: Validated apps in isolated sandbox (Zero Production Impact).
  • Cutover: Final sync & switch DNS.

The Migration Checklist

04
