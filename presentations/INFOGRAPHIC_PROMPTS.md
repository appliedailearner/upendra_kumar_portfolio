# Infographic Generation Prompts

Use these prompts with your preferred AI image generation tool (e.g., DALL-E 3, Midjourney, or "Google Nano Banana Pro") to create the PNG infographics for your presentations.

## General Style Guide
**Style:** Corporate, High-End Executive Presentation, Flat Design with Isomeric elements.
**Color Palette:** Microsoft Azure Blue (#0078D4), Dark Navy (#002050), White, and Light Grey.
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (Landscape).
**Text:** Minimal text, focus on icons and flow.

---

## 1. Data Center Migration (01-presentation-datacenter-migration.md)
**Filename:** `01-datacenter-migration-journey.webp`
**Prompt:**
> Create a professional business infographic showing a "Data Center Migration Journey". Left side: A stylized, slightly grayed-out on-premises server rack. Middle: A glowing blue path flowing through a "Migration Hub" icon. Right side: A modern, bright cloud city infrastructure representing Azure. Connect them with an arrow labeled "Modernization Path". Highlight a badge on the right saying "30% Cost Savings". Style: Clean, isometric vector art, white background, corporate blue accents.

## 2. Azure Landing Zone (02-presentation-landing-zone.md)
**Filename:** `02-landing-zone-governance.webp`
**Prompt:**
> Create a professional business infographic illustrating an "Azure Landing Zone Architecture". Center: A large shield icon representing "Governance & Security". Surrounding it: Connected nodes representing "Identity", "Network", and "Management". Below: A foundation layer labeled "Compliance". Visual style: Hub-and-spoke network diagram, clean lines, secure and stable feel. Colors: Azure blue, shield in gold or green to signify safety.

## 3. Well-Architected Review (03-presentation-well-architected.md)
**Filename:** `03-well-architected-cycle.webp`
**Prompt:**
> Create a professional business infographic showing a "Continuous Optimization Cycle". A circular flow diagram with three main stages: 1. "Assess" (Magnifying glass icon), 2. "Remediate" (Wrench/Tools icon), 3. "Monitor" (Dashboard/Chart icon). In the center of the loop, place a diamond shape labeled "Value". Style: Modern circular process chart, gradients of blue and teal, clean white background.

## 4. App Modernization (04-presentation-app-modernization.md)
**Filename:** `04-app-modernization-pipeline.webp`
**Prompt:**
> Create a professional business infographic depicting an "Application Modernization Pipeline". Left: A clunky, monolithic cube structure. Arrow pointing right through a "DevOps Loop" (infinity symbol). Right: A streamlined, modular set of floating containers or hexagons representing "Microservices". Visualizing the transformation from heavy/slow to light/fast. Style: Tech-forward, dynamic motion, neon blue and purple accents on white.

## 5. AI & Analytics (05-presentation-ai-analytics.md)
**Filename:** `05-ai-analytics-value-chain.webp`
**Prompt:**
> Create a professional business infographic showing the "Data-to-Value Chain". Left: Scattered puzzle pieces representing "Raw Data". Middle: A funnel or processing engine representing "Azure Synapse/AI". Right: A glowing lightbulb or upward trending chart representing "Business Insights". The flow should look magical and transformative. Style: High-tech, data streams, particles, polished and premium look.
