AI + Analytics Platform Enablement

Secure, scalable foundation for governed data and production AI

Industry: E-Commerce

My role: Infrastructure + AI Delivery Architect

Data platform owner: Data Architect (Fabric, pipelines, Power BI, SQL, Data Factory, ETL)

What the client needed

  • A governed analytics platform that could scale with growth and seasonal peaks.
  • Private and compliant access to data and AI services.
  • A clear operating model so platform work did not turn into ad-hoc requests.
  • A delivery plan that reduced risk and enabled incremental rollout.

What I owned (Infrastructure + AI delivery)

  • Landing zone alignment: subscription strategy, resource groups, naming, policy baseline.
  • Network and access design: hub-spoke connectivity, private endpoints, DNS, controlled egress.
  • Security controls: Entra ID RBAC, Key Vault, secrets management, least privilege patterns.
  • AI platform enablement: environment setup for AI workloads, model access patterns, secure API exposure.
  • Reliability and operations: logging/monitoring approach, DR considerations, runbooks and support handover.
  • Delivery governance: pilot-first rollout, risks/assumptions, release gates, stakeholder reporting.
  • Cost controls: tagging strategy, budgets, guardrails to prevent spend surprises.

What the Data Architect owned (Data platform build)

  • Data pipelines and orchestration (ETL/ELT jobs).
  • Microsoft Fabric (OneLake, Lakehouse/Warehouse) and workspace structure.
  • Power BI semantic models and dashboards.
  • SQL layer and curated datasets.
  • Data Factory / Fabric pipelines and integration patterns.
  • Data quality checks and operational scheduling for jobs.

Architecture view (high level)

  • Foundation: CAF-aligned landing zone, policy baseline, standardized subscriptions.
  • Connectivity: hub-spoke, private routing, DNS control, private endpoints for PaaS services.
  • Identity: Entra ID groups, RBAC model by persona (platform, data engineers, BI, consumers).
  • Security: Key Vault, secrets rotation approach, audit-friendly access reviews.
  • AI integration: secure access path for AI services and APIs, with throttling and policy controls.

Delivery approach (pilot-first)

  • Phase 0: platform readiness, access model, network/security baseline, governance setup.
  • Phase 1: onboard first data domain and first dashboards on the governed foundation.
  • Phase 2: enable AI use cases on approved datasets with secure access patterns.
  • Phase 3: scale to more domains, standardize templates, operationalize and hand over.

Outcomes (what changed)

  • Platform foundation enabled safe rollout of analytics and AI without rework.
  • Clear separation of responsibilities reduced delivery friction across infra and data teams.
  • Private connectivity and RBAC reduced exposure risk for sensitive datasets.
  • Governance and cost controls made the platform predictable to operate.

Artifacts I delivered

  • High-level architecture and connectivity design.
  • Security and access model (RBAC + private access patterns).
  • Environment readiness plan and rollout sequencing.
  • Operational guidance and handover notes.
